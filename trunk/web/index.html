FUTIL: FOAF Utils

FUTIL was born with the idea to make more useful (for humans and machines) all the semantic information stored in the millions of FOAFs published in the WWW. Initially to satisfy information puntual necessities of SWAML project, but without forgetting others outside the scope of that project.

Experiments

We are making some practical experiments related with FOAF.

The conclusions will be applied to develop some services to make more useful all the (semantic) information described in FOAF profiles.
